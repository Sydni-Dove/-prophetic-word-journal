How to Use This Generator
  1. 1
    Upload or paste your notes Drag in a PDF, Word doc, or TXT file, or paste your prophetic notes into the text box above. You can also skip this step and fill in the fields manually.
  2. 2
    Generate and review The generator reads your notes, fills in the journal fields, and opens a card-based review so you can confirm the content before continuing to print.
  3. 3
    Review before printing Use "Back to Form" to adjust any field and generate again to refresh the review cards. In Print view, use the page nav to jump between entries.
  4. 4
    Adjust output settings in Print Use the Print stage to choose page layout, theme colors, and notes-page options without crowding the initial content form.
  5. 5
    Continue to print Move from Review to Print when the generated pages look right. The print stage keeps the same page structure used in Review.
  6. 6
    Save as PDF Downloads a formatted PDF directly to your computer — no print dialog needed. You can also use the Print button and choose "Save as PDF" as the destination for the highest-quality output.
Tip: If you paste multiple dated entries (e.g. 2.23.26 … 2.24.26 …), the generator automatically creates a separate journal page for each date.
